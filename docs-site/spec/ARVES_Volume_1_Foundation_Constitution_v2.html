ARVES Volume-1: Foundation Constitution v2.0

STATUS: FOUNDATIONAL CONSTITUTION (FROZEN AFTER APPROVAL)

1. Executive Summary

ARVES is a Tenant-Aware Universal Intelligence Platform. Its purpose is to transform information into knowledge, knowledge into thinking, thinking into direction, direction into action, and action into learning.

2. Purpose

ARVES is not a chatbot, RAG system, workflow engine, or agent framework. It is a foundational intelligence platform capable of supporting people, teams, organizations, agents, and devices.

3. Mission

Mission: Any Information → Canonical Knowledge → Thinking → Direction → Action → Learning.

4. Vision

Build a Universal Intelligence Platform that can power Personal AI, Team AI, Enterprise AI, Multi-Agent Systems, and future Embodied Intelligence systems.

5. Universal Intelligence Theory

World → Information → Knowledge → Thinking → Direction → Action → Experience → Learning → World. This cycle is the foundation of ARVES.

6. Seven Fundamental Questions

Who am I? → Identity What do I know? → Information How do I think? → Cognitive Where should I go? → Strategic How do I interact? → Experience How do I improve? → Evolution How do I act? → Embodied

7. Platform Constitution

Tenant Layer Identity & Governance Layer Information Core Cognitive Core Strategic Core Experience Core Evolution Core Embodied Core

8. Universal Capability Model

Foundation Intelligence Strategic Intelligence Experience Intelligence Evolution Intelligence Emotional & Social Intelligence Life Operating System Intelligence Multi-Agent Intelligence Embodied Intelligence

9. Design Principles

Knowledge First Provider Agnostic AI Native Ontology Driven Event Driven Tenant First Contract First Security by Design

10. Architectural Principles

Separation of Concerns Single Ownership Explicit Contracts Composability Observability Resilience Evolvability

11. Information Philosophy

The goal of the Information Core is to transform Any Information into Canonical Knowledge.

12. Cognition Philosophy

The goal of the Cognitive Core is to transform Knowledge into Thinking through Context, Intent, Memory, Reasoning, Risk, Decision and Reflection.

13. Strategy Philosophy

The goal of the Strategic Core is to transform Thinking into Direction through Goals, Planning, Tradeoff Analysis and Resource Allocation.

14. Experience Philosophy

The goal of the Experience Core is to transform Direction into Human Interaction through Workspaces, Search, Voice, Presence and Conversation.

15. Evolution Philosophy

The goal of the Evolution Core is to transform Action into Learning through Reflection, Calibration, Meta-Learning and Knowledge Improvement.

16. Embodiment Philosophy

The goal of the Embodied Core is to transform Digital Intelligence into Physical Action through Vision, Sensors, Navigation and Robotics.

17. Core Relationships

Information → Cognitive Cognitive → Strategic Strategic → Experience Experience → Evolution Evolution → Information

18. Universal Tenant Model

Every intelligence operation must occur within a tenant and workspace boundary. No scope means no execution.

19. Non Goals

ARVES is not merely: - A chatbot - A copilot - A RAG system - An agent framework - An enterprise search system - A workflow engine

20. Success Definition

Information → Knowledge Knowledge → Thinking Thinking → Direction Direction → Action Action → Learning

The cycle must operate continuously, safely, observably, and at scale.

21. Final Definition

ARVES is a Tenant-Aware Universal Intelligence Platform for Persons, Teams, Organizations, Agents, and Devices.

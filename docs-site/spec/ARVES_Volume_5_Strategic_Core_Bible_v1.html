ARVES Volume-5: Strategic Core Bible v1.0

STATUS: CORE CONSTITUTION (FROZEN AFTER APPROVAL)

Part 1 — Purpose

Transform Thinking into Direction.

Part 2 — Strategic Philosophy

Strategy is the combination of goals, plans, tradeoffs and priorities.

Part 3 — Strategic Runtime

Observe → Evaluate → Prioritize → Plan → Allocate → Execute → Measure → Adapt.

Part 4 — Goal Constitution

A Goal is a desired future state. Goal types include Personal, Team, Organization, Agent and System goals.

Part 5 — Goal Hierarchy

Vision → Objective → Goal → Milestone → Task.

Part 6 — Goal Lifecycle

Create → Prioritize → Plan → Execute → Measure → Adapt → Complete.

Part 7 — Planning Engine

Convert goals into executable plans.

Part 8 — Task Decomposition

Goal → Plan → Task → Action.

Part 9 — Opportunity Intelligence

Detect opportunities and strategic advantages.

Part 10 — Tradeoff Engine

Evaluate cost, value, risk, time and resource tradeoffs.

Part 11 — Resource Allocation

Allocate time, money, people, agents and compute resources.

Part 12 — Scenario Simulation

Evaluate possible futures and strategic outcomes.

Part 13 — Priority Arbitration

Resolve competing priorities and conflicts.

Part 14 — Constraint Intelligence

Reason about budget, time, policy, resources and risk constraints.

Part 15 — Strategy Memory

Store goals, plans, outcomes and lessons learned.

Part 16 — Strategic Risk

Analyze long-term failure and strategic uncertainty.

Part 17 — Compounding Strategy

Maximize long-term value and cumulative advantage.

Part 18 — Strategic Observability

Track goal progress, plan progress, execution success and ROI.

Part 19 — Strategic Ownership

Owns Goals, Plans, Strategies, Simulations, Priorities and Resource Allocation.

Part 20 — Strategic Runtime Flow

Goals → Planning → Tradeoffs → Simulation → Prioritization → Allocation → Execution → Measurement.

Part 21 — Strategic Contracts

Goal Contract, Planning Contract, Strategy Contract and Simulation Contract.

Part 22 — Success Criteria

Thinking must be transformed into clear, measurable and adaptive direction.

Part 23 — Final Definition

Strategic Core = ARVES Direction Engine.
